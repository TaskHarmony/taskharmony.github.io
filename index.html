How TaskHarmony Works

Our platform makes voice bot testing simple, efficient, and powerful.

1

Connect Your Voice Bot

Integrate your voice bot with our platform using our SDK or direct API connections to Vapi, Retell, LiveKit, or Pipecat. Setup takes just minutes with our guided process.

2

Define Test Scenarios

Create test scripts or use our library of pre-built scenarios. Define expected responses, conversation flows, and edge cases that your voice bot should handle.

3

Run Tests at Scale

Execute tests individually or in batches. Our platform can simulate thousands of concurrent users to stress-test your voice bot under real-world conditions.

4

Analyze Results

Review detailed performance metrics, conversation logs, and failure points. Our dashboard provides intuitive visualizations to quickly identify areas for improvement.

5

Iterate and Improve

Use our insights to refine your voice bot. Rerun tests to verify improvements and ensure your bot performs consistently across all scenarios.
